Common Questions

Everything you need to know

What happens on the discovery call?

It's a free 15-minute chat. We'll talk about your idea, figure out which track fits best, and decide together if working together makes sense. No pitch, no hard sell - just a real conversation about what you want to build.

Do I need any technical experience?

None. That's the whole point. AI does the heavy lifting - I teach you how to direct it effectively. If you can describe what you want, we can build it.

What can I actually build in 2 weeks?

A professional website, a working mobile app prototype, or a launched business with a live landing page and clear strategy. What's realistic depends on your idea and your track - which is exactly what we figure out on the discovery call.

How are the sessions structured?

You get dedicated 1-on-1 sessions with me, multiple times per week. We work together live - I guide, you build. It's hands-on every session, not me talking at you while you take notes. You'll be doing real work from the first session.

What's included for free with the LaunchPad?

The AI Playbook (our $19 prompting guide), 3 months of the SkillSurge Community ($147 value), and the AI Starter Guide. You keep all of these even after the LaunchPad ends - so you walk away with the product you built plus ongoing resources.

What if I'm not sure what I want to build?

That's one of our four tracks. A lot of people come in with "I want to build something but I'm not sure what yet" - and that's totally fine. That's exactly what the discovery call and the first session are for. We'll figure it out together.

Only 10 spots per round - is that real?

Yes. I work with people 1-on-1, so I genuinely can't take more than 10 at a time without the quality dropping. When a round fills up, you wait for the next one. If you're interested, book a discovery call sooner rather than later.
